## Product Title
Quick Camera Profile (QCP)

## Short Description (store card)
Create high-quality camera profiles in minutes. QCP automates ColorChecker detection and builds ICC (Capture One) and DCP (Lightroom/ACR) profiles from RAW files.

## Product Description (full)
Quick Camera Profile is a desktop app for photographers and color-focused creators who need reliable, repeatable camera profiling without command-line workflow.

What you get:
- Automatic ColorChecker detection with corner pin refinement
- Fast profile creation from RAW images
- Capture One ICC output and Lightroom/ACR DCP output
- Camera-aware profile naming and install-ready export
- Local desktop workflow (no cloud upload required)

Ideal for studios, product photographers, and creators who need consistent color across cameras and shoots.
